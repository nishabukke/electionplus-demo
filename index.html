Looking at the current political system, it is seen that new developments are happening rapidly every day. The cycle of power has sometimes fallen in the hands of the BJP, sometimes in the hands of the Nationalists, and sometimes it has stopped in front of the Sena. As per our constitutional provisions, the Governor, as the representative of the President, administers the oath of office of the Chief Minister to the majority leader who claims to be the Chief Minister.

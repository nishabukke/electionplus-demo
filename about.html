About Election +

Given the current political situation, social media has become an important medium for political candidates to campaign and disseminate. And keeping this main thing in mind, Election Plus is providing you the accurate data of your constituency, its ward wise leaders and the information about the improvements made in that area in just one click. Having accurate data of constituencies makes the task of determining the target voters in that area easier. From quality service at low cost with best use of speed and efficiency, Election Plus works to plan the right strategy according to the candidate's budget.
